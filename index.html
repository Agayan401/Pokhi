About Pokhi

The skies of Assam are filled with incredible birdlife, but their true local identities are rarely found in field guides.

To celebrate and preserve this heritage, this growing directory pairs high-quality field photography and quick habitat facts with authentic Assamese names, creating a visual catalog of the birds in our own backyards.

Whether you are a seasoned birder, a student, or simply someone who loves Assam's natural heritage, we invite you to explore and use the feedback section to help our community catalog grow!
